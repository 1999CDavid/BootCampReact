Who we are

We are a group passionate about soccer and, in particular, FC Barcelona. We came together because of our admiration and love for this team, which has thrilled and conquered us with its style of play and competitive spirit. Our goal is to share this passion with other Barça fans and create a community where we can support our team together, share our opinions and emotions, and enjoy the experience of being culés.

As a fan club, we strive to uphold the values of FC Barcelona, which are excellence, respect, humility and social commitment, humility and social commitment. We believe that soccer is more than a sport, it is a tool to promote positive values and unite people from different backgrounds and cultures. positive values and unite people from different backgrounds and cultures.

If you are a fan of FC Barcelona and share our passion and values, we invite you to join our fan club. Together we can cheer the team through thick and thin, celebrate their triumphs and learn from their defeats, and enjoy the thrill of football in good company. Visit us on our social networks or at our meetings and become part of the culé family!

History

Less than a month ago, a group of friends gathered in a bar to watch FC Barcelona play Real Madrid. They had all been Barça fans since they were kids and had followed the team for years, but had never had the opportunity to watch a game together live. The excitement of the clásico and the atmosphere in the bar made them feel a special connection with each other, and they realized they wanted to continue sharing that passion and excitement together.

That's how they decided to create an FC Barcelona fan club. Although at first there were only a few of them, it didn't take long for them to spread the word through social networks and chat groups of friends. Soon, other Barça fans joined the group.

Since then, members have gathered at every Barça match via the web, interacting with everything about the Barca universe, organized debates, screenings of documentaries and historical Barça matches, and discussed the latest team news. Although the fan club is still young, the members are excited to continue growing and strengthening their culé community. Together, they hope to experience great moments and share the excitement of soccer with other FC Barcelona fans.
